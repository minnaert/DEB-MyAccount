Brokers and Third Party Access

Brokers and their customers can both use My Account to manage their Direct Energy Business accounts.

We have reorganized how energy brokers and third party payers can work with their customers and be approved to have access to their customers' information. This automated system replaces a legacy paper-based process and will ease your customer onboarding process.

  • Brokers and Third Party Payers will keep their access to all their existing customers, just as they do today. New brokers and third party payers will need to register with those roles by using the Advanced Registration option on the registration page.
  • New Customers will register just as they do today, but can choose to grant access to their accounts.
  • Customers can grant access to their accounts without registering for My Account by using the link on business.directenergy.com provided by the broker or third party payer.
