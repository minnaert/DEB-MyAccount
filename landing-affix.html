BRAND PROMISE, POSITIONING AND REASONS TO BELIEVE

WHY

Help make your business better

Our brand promise communicates why we exist and describes the single greatest benefit we provide to our customers.


HOW

Buy less of what we sell

Our brand positioning communicates how we deliver on our brand promise. It’s the thing we do that is different from everyone else in our industry and should be part of almost every message we put in the marketplace.


REASONS TO BELIEVE

Reasons to believe provide the proof that we can do for our customers we promise. They explain the tangible features and benefits that support our brand positioning.

Data & Analytics

  • Device-level monitoring
  • System-level visibility
  • Comprehensive reporting
  • Actionable consumption recommendations

Advisory Services

  • Total energy management strategies
  • Personalized, customized consulting
  • Prioritization and recommendations
  • Savings reporting and analytics

Energy Efficiency

  • Lighting and light control upgrades
  • HVAC retrofits
  • Piping or ducting installation
  • Water conservation
  • Temperature controls

Distributed Generation

  • Solar panel installation financing
  • Energy storage

Demand Response

  • Consumption monitoring and analytics
  • Curtailment strategies


None of these statements are taglines. They will not appear under our logo or as stand-alone statements without context. That said, they both should be used in content and should be supported by features or benefits of our products or experience.
