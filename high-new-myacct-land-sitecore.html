Access All of Your Account Information

You will have has access to all of your important account information.

  • If you have multiple accounts, you will be able to easily switch between your accounts and also see a consolidated view across all of your accounts, including gas and power.
  • Your power and gas accounts are grouped and easily distinguished. Choose to view all of your accounts, just power or natural gas, or select a single account.
  • For those users with access to many customers and accounts, there are powerful search features which allow you to manage just the accounts you need to.
  • The account list displays only those accounts which apply to what is selected in the customer list.

Profiles

You and your customers can now add, edit, and rearrange profiles to create meaningful groups of the accounts you have access to

  • Group your customers into meaningful sets.
  • You can create profiles with any combination of customers that you have access to.
  • To add customers or maintain profiles, select Preferences in the top-right of the page and then select the Customers tab. This page has a section for adding customers to your username if they are not already added and a Profiles section where you can create, clone, edit or delete profiles.
  • After you have created your first profile, a Profiles section will appear in the customer list.
  • Select a profile in the customer list to filter the accounts list to only include those accounts which apply. The rest of the information on the page will be updated show only the information with applies to the selected customers and accounts.

Understand Your Usage Better

Updated Usage Chart

The updated usage chart allows you to compare your current year usage with the previous years usage. By clicking on each series in the legend, you can decide which information to show. The usage chart also has a printable view, which lets you include the chart of any reporting needs. The chart on the Usage tab has been updated to include a year over year comparison. Usage and Spend for both the current year and previous year are displayed. Users can select which information to view.

The summary page still displays a rolling 13 month usage chart.

Learn more about creating usage or consumption reports through our how to video.

Updated Energy Market Alerts

NYMEX Alerts have been renamed to Energy Market Alerts. In the first release only monthly alerts could be created. With this release Strip and Weighted Average Strip pricing alerts can be created as well. Energy Market Alerts are a valuable tool for customers who want to track commodity price trends in a time efficient manner. When an Energy Market Alert is set and if a market price exceeds the high set price or goes below the low set price, customers will receive emails and messages in the My Account Message Center.
